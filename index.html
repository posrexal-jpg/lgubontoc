About Bontoc

A Community Built on Tradition & Progress

Bontoc is a vibrant municipality nestled in the highlands of South Cotabato, Philippines. Known for its rich cultural heritage, scenic landscapes, and warm, industrious people, Bontoc continues to grow as a model of sustainable local governance.

Our local government is committed to delivering efficient public services, fostering economic development, and preserving the environment for future generations.
